Services

Music Production

Producers: Working with producers who guide the artistic and technical process of creating a song or album. Producers help shape the sound, arrangement, and overall vision of the music.
Arrangement and Composition: Assisting with song arrangement, orchestration, and composition to enhance the musicality and appeal of the tracks.

Studio Access and Engineering

Recording Studios: Providing access to high-quality recording studios equipped with advanced technology and soundproof environments.
Audio Engineering: Employing skilled audio engineers who manage the technical aspects of recording, including microphone placement, sound mixing, and ensuring optimal audio quality.

Mixing and Mastering

Mixing: Balancing individual tracks (vocals, instruments) to create a cohesive and polished sound. Mixing involves adjusting levels, adding effects, and ensuring clarity and cohesion.
Mastering: The final step in the production process where the mixed tracks are fine-tuned for optimal playback quality across all platforms and formats. Mastering includes equalization, compression, and ensuring consistency between tracks.

Got Questions?

How do I submit my music or get signed by your label?

Submit your music through our online form, including a demo, artist bio, and press materials. We review submissions and contact promising artists for further discussions.

What support and services do you offer to signed artists?

We provide artist development, studio access, production services, marketing and promotion, distribution, and legal and financial management.

How does your label promote new releases and handle marketing?

We promote new releases through digital marketing, social media campaigns, press outreach, and promotional events, including music videos and visual content.

What does the production process involve and can independent artists use your studios?

Production includes recording, mixing, mastering, and sometimes songwriting. Studio access is primarily for signed artists, but we occasionally offer studio time to independents.

How are sales, royalties, and financial matters managed?

We handle distribution across various platforms, track sales and royalties, and provide transparent accounting and regular payments based on contract terms.

How can I contact your label or arrange a visit?

Contact us via our website’s contact form, email, or phone. Visits to our offices or studios are by appointment; please reach out in advance to schedule.
